Who We Are

One Tribe. One Mission.

Jesus Tribe — The Teen Expression of TCN Abuja

Jesus Tribe is the official teenagers' ministry of The Covenant Nation (TCN), Abuja — a vibrant community where teenagers discover purpose, build authentic friendships, and encounter God in a way that's real, relevant, and unforgettable.

The Jesus Tribe Summer Camp is our flagship annual gathering — packed with high‑energy worship, life‑shaping sessions, games, creativity, and genuine connection. This is Edition 4, and it's set to be the biggest yet.

Whether it's your first camp or your fourth, you belong here. Come as you are — leave transformed.
